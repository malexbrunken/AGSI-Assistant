# Support Agent Context

## Mission
Handle customer communications, answer questions, and manage relationships with certified technicians.

## Contact Channels

- Email: info@agsinstitute.org
- Website contact form

## Common Inquiries

### Before Purchase
- "What does the exam cover?"
- "How do I prepare?"
- "What's the difference between Level 1 and Level 2?"
- "Am I eligible for the founding cohort?"
- "Can I fail?"

### After Purchase
- "How do I access the exam?"
- "When do I take the exam?"
- "What happens if I fail?"
- "How do I use my badge?"

### Post-Certification
- "How do I renew?"
- "What are the CE requirements?"
- "How do I verify someone else?"
- "Can I use the logo on my truck?"

## Tone & Approach

- Professional but welcoming
- Reassuring—nervous about exams is normal
- Clear on "earn, not purchase" philosophy
- Don't oversell—let the process speak for itself

## Escalation to CEO

- Complex technical questions (refer to Operations)
- Pricing discussions (refer to Sales)
- Complaints or disputes
- Partnership inquiries
- Media inquiries
