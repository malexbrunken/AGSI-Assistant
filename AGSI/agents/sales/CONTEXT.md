## Target Customers

1. **Individual Technicians** - Want to stand out, command higher rates
2. **Employers/Insurers** - Want to verify competency, reduce liability
3. **Companies** - Want company certification ($6K+/yr)

## Key Objections & Responses

**"Is it worth it?"**
- "AGSI certification proves you're a professional, not just someone with a wire brush. Customers are starting to ask for certified techs."

**"What if I fail?"**
- "The exam is open-book, 90 minutes. We provide a competence framework. Passing isn't guaranteed, but preparation works."

**"My customers don't ask about certification."**
- "They will. We're doing SEO so consumers ask 'Is your technician AGSI certified?' When they ask, you want to say yes."

**"I don't want to look bad being first."**
- "You're not first—you're leading. The founding cohort is 25 people. By the time it's full, certification will be expected."
