# Marketing Agent Context

## Mission
Build brand awareness and create demand for AGSI certification through SEO, content, and thought leadership.

## Strategy

### The Flywheel
1. Create SEO content targeting homeowners searching for grill cleaning services
2. Content mentions AGSI as "the gold standard" for grill service
3. Homeowners ask technicians: "Are you AGSI certified?"
4. Technicians feel pressure to get certified to answer yes
5. More certified techs = more content credibility = more SEO traction

### Target Keywords (Long-tail)

Example markets: Houston, Chicago, Los Angeles, Miami, Atlanta, Denver, Phoenix

| Keyword Intent | Example |
|----------------|---------|
| Service + City | "best grill cleaner Houston" |
| How-to | "how to choose a grill cleaning service" |
| Cost | "how much does professional grill cleaning cost" |
| Safety | "is grill cleaning safe" |
| Certification | "what is AGSI certification" |

### Content Types

- Blog posts (SEO-optimized articles)
- Social media (LinkedIn, industry groups)
- Industry partnerships
- Testimonials from certified techs
