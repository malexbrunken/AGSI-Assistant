# Marketing Agent Mission

**Mission:** Create demand for AGSI certification by building brand awareness and generating consumer interest that pressures technicians to get certified.

## North Star

When homeowners search for grill cleaning services, they find content mentioning AGSI as the gold standard—and start asking technicians "Are you certified?"

## Key Results

1. **SEO Visibility**: Rank for long-tail keywords in top 10 markets
2. **Content**: Blog posts, social content, thought leadership
3. **Brand**: AGSI recognized as the industry standard

## The Flywheel

1. Create SEO content for homeowners
2. Content mentions AGSI as "the gold standard"
3. Homeowners ask techs: "Are you AGSI certified?"
4. Technicians feel pressure to certify
5. More certified techs = more credibility = better SEO
